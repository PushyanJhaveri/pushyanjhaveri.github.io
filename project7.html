Project Overview

Enhancing Customer Retention with Data-Driven Insights

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges businesses face today. A high churn rate leads to revenue loss, increased acquisition costs, and decreased customer lifetime value. This project focuses on predicting customer churn for one of our clients' at Fingertips using machine learning models and interactive data analysis, enabling businesses to identify at-risk customers and take proactive retention measures. This model was developed in 2021

Key Features:

  • A Fully Interactive Dashboard (built with Dash) that visualizes churn insights, customer segmentation, and feature importance.
  • A Composite Image Generator (using Plotly) that combines key visualizations into a single image for easy reporting.
  • Machine Learning Models that predict customer churn and provide insights into the key factors influencing it.
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to evaluate customer retention strategies.

Data Exploration & Preprocessing

The dataset used for this project includes:

  • Training Data: 5,000+ customer records with features such as Total Spend, Support Calls, Contract Length, Payment Delay, Age, Gender, Tenure, and more.
  • Testing Data: A separate dataset to validate the model’s predictive capabilities.

Key Data Insights

Churn Distribution:

  • ~27% of customers have churned, while ~73% remain active.
  • This imbalance suggests that a balanced accuracy approach should be considered while training the model.

Feature Correlations:

  • Support Calls & Churn: Customers who made more support calls had a higher likelihood of churning.
  • Contract Length & Churn: Customers with shorter contracts were more likely to churn.
  • Total Spend & Churn: Customers who spent less money were more likely to leave.

Customer Segmentation (Using KMeans Clustering)

High-Risk Customers: Frequent support calls, short contract lengths, and low total spend.

Medium-Risk Customers: Moderate interaction with support and average spending.

Low-Risk Customers: Long-term customers with high total spend and low support interaction.

Machine Learning Models & Performance

To predict churn, multiple models were tested:

Model Accuracy Precision Recall F1-Score AUC-ROC
Logistic Regression 84.2% 76.5% 72.3% 74.3% 0.82
Random Forest 91.3% 84.7% 81.2% 82.9% 0.91
XGBoost 93.5% 89.1% 87.3% 88.2% 0.95

Best Model: XGBoost

XGBoost achieved the highest accuracy (93.5%) and best ROC-AUC score (0.95), making it the most effective model for churn prediction.

It excels in handling non-linear relationships and feature interactions, making it ideal for customer churn analysis.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

This model evaluates churn reduction strategies using the following KPIs:

  • Churn Rate
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
  • Customer Engagement Score
  • Retention Rate

Data Visualization & Dashboard

1. Churn Analysis & Feature Correlations

Interactive visualizations include churn distribution, heatmaps, and feature importance scores to identify key churn factors.

2. Customer Segmentation

KMeans clustering groups customers into high, medium, and low-risk segments, enabling targeted retention efforts.

3. Predictive Model Insights

A dedicated tab in the dashboard showcases model performance metrics, confusion matrix, and precision-recall trade-offs.

4. Retention Strategies

Proactive actions are recommended based on churn probability, including:

  • Personalized offers for high-risk customers.
  • Improved customer support for those with frequent service issues.
  • Incentives for long-term contracts to boost customer retention.

Retention Strategy Implementation

Based on Churn Prediction, The Following Strategies are Recommended:

  1. Reducing Support Call Volume
  2. Incentivizing Long-Term Contracts
  3. Personalized Offers for At-Risk Customers
  4. Predictive Monitoring of Customer Behavior

How This Project Helps Businesses

  • Increases Customer Retention Rates – Businesses can take action before customers churn.
  • Reduces Operational Costs – Retaining customers is 5x cheaper than acquiring new ones.
  • Maximizes Revenue Growth – A 1% increase in retention can lead to a 5-10% increase in profits.
  • Enhances Customer Satisfaction – Addressing churn reasons improves customer experience.

Conclusion

This project successfully analyzed, predicted, and visualized customer churn while providing actionable business strategies to reduce churn rates and retain customers. With a combination of data-driven insights, machine learning, and interactive dashboards, businesses can improve customer relationships, increase profitability, and stay competitive in a rapidly evolving market.
